Challenges

Product Expansion & Rediscovering the Audience

After years of extreme growth Leesa hit a pivotal point to expand its product offerings by introducing a hybrid foam & spring mattress and other essential sleep products aimed at providing an entire bedroom solution. This major change in site infrastructure pushed us to create new user flows and marketing strategies to match our evolving site.

After launching a new site with two core (mattress) products marketed with different prices and features, we found another challenge to understand what drives our changing audience, so that we could curate and optimize the ads, content and site experiences needed for conversions.

Evolving the Brand & Creating a Digital Sanctuary

In mid-2018, Leesa also transformed its brand & messaging to differentiate itself in a newly crowded market by putting a focus on the bedroom as a sanctuary to rest, recharge and recover. In doing so, we created an elevated eCommerce experience using emotional, humanistic language & photography with purposeful shift towards resonating with a larger female audience.
